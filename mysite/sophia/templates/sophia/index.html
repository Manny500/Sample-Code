The Project

A database capable of integrating different data files relating to education data. The education data may vary in content, however, in general they will consist of different metrics assessing a school's performance, along with identification markers of the group of students such as ethnicity and grade level. The data is anonymous, there are no set identification markers that could be used to identify a student in real life. No individual student-level data is included in the database.
